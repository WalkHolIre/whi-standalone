Uw Vragen over Wandeltochten in Ierland Beantwoord

Wat als ik verdwaal?

We provide detailed route notes and GPS files on your phone. Our well-marked routes are tested multiple times, and you can call us anytime for support.

Hoe werkt de bagagetransfer?

Leave your main luggage at your B&B each morning — we collect and deliver it to your next stop. You walk with just a daypack.

Kan ik alleen wandelen?

Absolutely! Many walkers travel solo. Walk at your own pace and meet fellow walkers and locals at the B&Bs each evening.

Hoe is het weer?

Changeable — sunshine, showers, and rainbows all in one day! We recommend layers and waterproof gear. Check our blog for packing tips.

Welke accommodatie gebruikt u?

Family-run B&Bs personally selected and visited by us. Comfortable, warm, and welcoming with hearty Irish breakfasts.

Hoe ver van tevoren moet ik boeken?

8–12 weeks for peak season (May–September). Off-season tours can be booked with less notice. Earlier booking means better B&B choice.
