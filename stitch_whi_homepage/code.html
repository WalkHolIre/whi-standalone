Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know before you lace up your boots.

What if I get lost? expand_more
Don't worry! We provide detailed waterproof route notes, physical maps, and digital GPS files for your phone. Plus, our local support team is available 24/7 if you ever need help.
How does luggage transfer work? expand_more
Each morning, you leave your bags at the reception of your B&B by 9:30 AM. While you walk with just a daypack, our drivers move your luggage to your next accommodation. It's usually waiting for you when you arrive!
Can I walk solo? expand_more
Absolutely! Many of our walkers travel solo. The trails are well-marked and Ireland is a very safe destination for solo travelers. Some B&Bs may have a small single supplement.
What's the weather like? expand_more
Irish weather is famous for its "four seasons in one day." We recommend layering and good waterproof gear. Our walking season runs from March to October when days are longer and weather is generally milder.
What accommodation do you use? expand_more
We use handpicked, family-run B&Bs and guesthouses known for their hospitality and quality. All rooms are en-suite, and a hearty Irish breakfast is included every morning.
How far in advance should I book? expand_more
We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially for popular routes like the Dingle Way during the summer months, as accommodation can fill up quickly.
