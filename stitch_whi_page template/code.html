The Story of Cliff & Louise

Founded by Cliff and Louise, Walking Holiday Ireland was born from a shared passion for the rugged, untamed beauty of the Irish landscape. What started as a personal quest to map the hidden trails of the Emerald Isle has blossomed into a premier walking tour experience.

With over 13 years of curated experience, we pride ourselves on providing intimate, expert-led tours that go beyond the beaten path. We believe that Ireland is best seen at 3 miles per hour—where the stories of the land, the folklore, and the warmth of the people can truly be felt.

"Our mission is simple: to share the Ireland we love with people who value authenticity and connection to nature."
