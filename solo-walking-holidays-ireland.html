Walk at your own pace. Sleep in handpicked B&Bs each night. Your bags are already there.

How Inn-to-Inn Works

1

Walk Point A to B

Start your day with a light daypack. Follow the trail at your own pace.

2

Bags Moved for You

Your luggage is collected from your B&B and transported to the next one.

3

Arrive at Next B&B

Your bags are waiting at your next village accommodation.

4

Rest & Repeat

Enjoy the village pub, meet other walkers, then do it all again tomorrow.

Each night a different village. Each night a different pub. Each night a different welcome. That's the magic of walking Ireland solo.
