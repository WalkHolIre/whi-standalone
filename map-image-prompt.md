# AI Image Prompt — Ireland Walking Regions Map

## Prompt

Create a clean, modern illustrated map of the island of Ireland (Republic of Ireland and Northern Ireland) showing four colour-coded tourism regions. The map should be professional, minimal, and designed for use on a travel website.

**Style:** Flat illustration, soft gradients, no photorealism. Clean vector look similar to a Fáilte Ireland tourism map. Light ocean background. Subtle terrain texture within each region (hills, coastline hints) but keeping it clean and readable. No cluttered detail — this is a brand asset, not a geography textbook.

**Colour palette (strict brand colours):**
- Wild Atlantic Way region (entire west coast, from Donegal down through Galway, Clare, Kerry to West Cork): **#F17E00** (warm orange)
- Ireland's Ancient East region (eastern half, from Dundalk/Cooley down through Dublin, Wicklow, Carlow/Kilkenny, Wexford, Waterford): **#3F0F87** (deep purple)
- Ireland's Hidden Heartlands (central midlands strip, Athlone/Shannon area): **#B58DB6** (soft mauve)
- Northern Ireland (the six counties, north): **#210747** (dark purple)
- Ocean/sea: light blue-grey, soft and muted
- Background outside Ireland: very light cream **#f8f7f5**

**Destination markers** — small orange (#F17E00) pin icons or dots with white labels at these locations:
- **Dingle** (Dingle Peninsula, southwest Kerry)
- **Kerry** (Iveragh Peninsula / Killarney area)
- **Burren** (north Clare, west coast)
- **Wicklow** (Wicklow Mountains, south of Dublin)
- **Barrow** (between Kildare and Waterford, southeast midlands)
- **Cooley** (Cooley Peninsula, north of Dundalk, near border)
- **Causeway** (north Antrim coast, Northern Ireland)

**Key cities to mark** (small, subtle, grey text — not dominant):
- Dublin (east coast)
- Belfast (Northern Ireland)
- Galway (west)
- Cork (south)
- Killarney (southwest)

**Region labels** — each region should have its name in white text, neatly placed within the region:
- "Wild Atlantic Way" (along the west coast)
- "Ireland's Ancient East" (in the eastern region)
- "Hidden Heartlands" (in the central strip)
- "Northern Ireland" (in the north)

**Legend** at the bottom showing the four regions with their colour swatches and names.

**Typography:** Clean sans-serif font (Inter or similar). Bold for region names, regular weight for city names and labels.

**Aspect ratio:** Portrait, roughly 3:4 (suitable for a website sidebar or card element, approximately 600×800px display size)

**Important:**
- The map should show the ENTIRE island of Ireland including Northern Ireland
- Regional boundaries should follow the official Fáilte Ireland tourism regions (see reference image)
- The Wild Atlantic Way route line along the west coast is a nice touch if possible (dashed white line along coastline)
- Keep it elegant and premium-feeling — this is for a high-end walking holiday company
- No cartoonish elements, no overly playful style
- The Barrow Way marker sits in the Ancient East (orange) region, NOT in Hidden Heartlands — it's between Kildare and Waterford

**Do NOT include:** Fáilte Ireland branding, any third-party logos, roads, or excessive geographic detail.
