How It Works

Everything arranged. You just walk.

4-Step Process

1

Choose Your Walk

Browse tours by destination, difficulty, or duration. We help you pick the perfect route for your fitness level, interests, and available time.

2

We Handle the Logistics

Accommodation booked at handpicked B&Bs. Luggage transferred daily. Route notes and GPS maps prepared. You carry a daypack — we handle everything else.

3

Walk at Your Pace

No group. No schedule. Wake up, have breakfast, walk. Stop for tea. Take photos. It's your day. Walk 20km or stop at 10 — it's your call.

4

Arrive to Comfort

Each evening, arrive at your next B&B with your bags already waiting. Freshen up and enjoy an evening in an Irish village.

The Self-Guided Difference

Self-guided means freedom. You're not locked into a group's pace or schedule. Walk 20km or stop at 10 — it's your call. But you're never truly alone: our team is a phone call away, your route is clearly marked, and your bags arrive before you do. It's the perfect balance between independence and support.
