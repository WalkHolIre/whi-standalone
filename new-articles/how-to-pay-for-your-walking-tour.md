# Book Your Irish Hiking Tour: Easy Payment & Booking Guide

Planning a walking holiday in Ireland is exciting, and understanding our booking and payment process will make securing your tour simple and straightforward. This guide explains everything you need to know about reserving and paying for your Irish walking adventure.

## Advance Planning and Booking Timeline

### Ideal Booking Window

We recommend booking **at least three weeks ahead** of your tour start date. However, booking **six weeks or more in advance** is ideal for several important reasons:

- **Limited Availability**: Popular tours, especially in peak season (June-August), have limited availability of accommodations
- **Better Selection**: Booking early ensures you have access to the best accommodation options
- **Peace of Mind**: Early booking allows ample time for confirmation, itinerary planning, and preparation
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Early bookers have more flexibility in choosing tour dates

Late bookings are possible but may be subject to availability and limited accommodation choices.

## Deposit and Payment Structure

### Initial Deposit Requirement

To secure your reservation and initiate our logistics arrangements, a **30% deposit** is required. This deposit:

- Confirms your place on the tour
- Initiates accommodation reservations with our partner hotels and guesthouses
- Begins logistics planning for your walking tour
- Is non-refundable under standard cancellation policies

### Full Payment Requirement for Late Bookings

If you book **within six weeks of your tour start date**, the full tour amount must be paid immediately upon booking. This is necessary because:

- Limited time for payment processing
- Accommodation must be confirmed immediately
- Logistical arrangements require immediate confirmation

### Invoice and Confirmation

After we receive your deposit:

1. We begin making formal reservations with accommodations and service providers
2. We send you a formal invoice detailing:
   - Your tour details and dates
   - Total tour cost
   - Deposit amount received
   - Remaining balance due
   - Payment deadline for final balance

### Payment Deadline for Final Balance

The remaining balance (70% of tour cost for standard bookings) must be received before the payment deadline specified in your invoice. Typical deadlines are:

- 42 days before tour commencement
- Earlier for late bookings

Failure to pay the balance by the deadline may result in tour cancellation with loss of the deposit.

## Payment Methods

Walking Holiday Ireland provides flexible payment options to suit your preferences and location.

### Online Payment Processing

**Stripe Payment Gateway**

All secure online payments are processed through **Stripe**, a globally recognized and trusted payment processor. Stripe provides:

- **High Security**: Advanced encryption and fraud protection
- **Multiple Options**: Credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets
- **Global Support**: Accepts cards from most countries
- **Instant Confirmation**: Immediate payment confirmation and receipt

**Accepted Payment Methods:**

When paying through Stripe, you can use:
- Visa and Mastercard credit cards
- Visa and Mastercard debit cards
- Digital wallets (where available)
- Bank payments (where available)

All payments are processed securely with SSL encryption to protect your financial information.

### Bank Transfer / SEPA Payments

For customers who prefer not to use online payment systems, we offer an alternative:

**SEPA Bank Transfer Option**

1. Select the "Bank Transfer" payment option during booking
2. You will receive our complete bank details via email
3. Transfer your payment directly from your bank account
4. Include your booking reference number in the transfer notes
5. Confirm payment receipt via email to info@walkingholidayireland.com

SEPA transfers typically process within 1-3 business days.

**Advantages of Bank Transfer:**

- No online processing fees for customers in many jurisdictions
- Direct bank-to-bank transaction
- Clear payment records in your banking statements
- Suitable for international payments from participating countries

### Currency and Exchange Rates

- Payments are processed in **Euros (EUR)**
- If paying from a different currency, your bank will calculate the exchange rate
- We recommend checking your bank's exchange rate to understand any applicable fees

## Tips for Booking Your Walking Tour

### 1. Check Tour Availability Early
Popular tours fill quickly, especially during peak season. Check availability before committing to travel dates.

### 2. Review Your Booking Confirmation
Ensure all details in your confirmation email are correct, including:
- Tour dates
- Number of participants
- Accommodation details
- Included services

### 3. Arrange Travel Insurance
We recommend arranging travel insurance immediately after booking to protect your investment against cancellation and other unexpected events.

### 4. Plan Your Travel Logistics
Consider booking flights and transportation to reach your tour starting point. Many tours begin in accessible locations like Dublin or Belfast.

### 5. Prepare Your Gear and Clothing
Use the time between booking and your tour to acquire necessary hiking gear and prepare physically for your walking adventure.
