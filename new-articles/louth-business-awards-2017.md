# Award-Winning Irish Hiking Tours: Recognized for Excellence

Walking Holiday Ireland was honored to be recognized as the 2017 Louth Business Awards Tourism winner. This prestigious award acknowledges the company's commitment to providing exceptional self-guided hiking tour experiences and promoting the natural wonders of Ireland to visitors from around the world.

## Award Ceremony and Recognition

The award was presented at the annual Gala dinner organized by Dundalk Chamber of Commerce, an event that brought together nearly 600 attendees celebrating business excellence in County Louth. The evening featured over 20 award categories, each recognizing outstanding businesses across various industries in the region.

### About the Award

The Louth Business Awards Tourism category recognizes businesses that demonstrate:

- Exceptional service quality to international and domestic visitors
- Innovation in tourism experiences
- Commitment to promoting the region's natural and cultural attractions
- Economic contribution to the local tourism industry
- Customer satisfaction and feedback excellence

Walking Holiday Ireland's recognition in this competitive category reflects the company's dedication to delivering outstanding self-guided walking holiday experiences throughout Ireland.

## Tour Offerings

Walking Holiday Ireland specializes in self-guided hiking experiences across multiple Irish landscapes, each offering unique scenery, cultural experiences, and outdoor adventures:

### Featured Walking Tours

**Wicklow Way**
- 131 kilometers of scenic trail through the Wicklow Mountains
- 5-10 day options available
- Moderate difficulty with diverse woodland and mountain scenery
- Close proximity to Dublin for easy access

**Cooley Peninsula**
- Coastal and mountain scenery in County Louth
- Historic sites and cultural heritage experiences
- Various tour lengths available
- Local flavor and off-the-beaten-path experiences

**Barrow Way**
- Follows Ireland's second-longest river
- 5-8 day self-guided tours
- Gentle terrain along waterside paths
- Charming villages and cultural experiences

**Causeway Coast**
- Northern Ireland's spectacular Atlantic coastline
- UNESCO World Heritage sites
- Dramatic cliff scenery and coastal walks
- 5-8 day tour options

**Kerry Way**
- 215 kilometers of scenic trail through County Kerry
- 8-10 day tours available
- Mountain and coastal scenery combined
- Access to historic sites and charming villages

**Dingle Way**
- Dingle Peninsula's scenic loop
- 5-6 day tour options
- Coastal and mountain terrain
- Rich cultural and historical significance
- Irish language and traditional music experiences

**The Burren**
- Unique limestone landscape in County Clare
- 3-5 day tours
- Distinctive geological formations
- Archaeological sites and natural features
