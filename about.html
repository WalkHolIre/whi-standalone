Founders Story

We're Cliff and Louise Wayenberg. In 2012, we turned our passion for Ireland's trails into Walking Holiday Ireland. What started as two hikers sharing routes with friends has grown into one of Ireland's most trusted walking holiday companies. But one thing hasn't changed — we still walk every single route, every single year.

Why Self-Guided

We believe the best way to experience Ireland is on foot, at your own pace. No groups to keep up with. No rigid schedule. Just you, the trail, and the extraordinary Irish landscape. Walk 20km or stop at 10 — it's your call. But you're never truly alone: our team is a phone call away, your route is clearly marked, and your bags arrive before you do.
