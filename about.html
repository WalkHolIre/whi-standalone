About Walking Holiday Ireland

Your trusted partner for unforgettable Irish hiking adventures

Why We Do What We Do

We believe the best way to experience Ireland is on foot — breathing in the salt air along rugged coastlines, following ancient paths through misty mountains, and discovering stories woven into every stone and valley. There's something magical about travelling at walking pace, where every turn reveals a new vista and every village offers a warm welcome.

At Walking Holiday Ireland, we handle all the logistics so you can simply walk, wonder, and wander. From carefully curated routes to handpicked accommodations and seamless baggage transfers, we take care of the details while you immerse yourself in the landscape. Our goal is simple: to give you the freedom to explore without the burden of planning.

Meet Cliff & Louise

Walking Holiday Ireland was founded by Cliff and Louise Waijenberg — avid hikers whose love for Ireland runs deep. Cliff brings his Dutch roots and a fresh perspective to the trails, while Louise grew up exploring the Dublin Mountains and knows these landscapes like old friends.

Together, they've walked countless routes across the country, from the heather-clad Wicklow Way to the dramatic Antrim Coast. Their passion? Sharing the Ireland they've fallen in love with — its mythology, its history, and its wild beauty. Every route they offer has been personally tested and refined to ensure it delivers an authentic Irish experience.

The couple were especially captivated by Cooley's rolling patchwork hills and the Mourne Mountains sweeping down to the sea. Here, ancient legends come alive: tales of the Táin Bó Cúailnge, the hero Cú Chulainn, and Viking longships on Carlingford Lough. Historic treasures abound nearby too — from the atmospheric ruins of Monasterboice and Mellifont Abbey to the ancient passage tomb at Newgrange, which predates the Egyptian pyramids by centuries.

Why Self-Guided?

We believe the best way to experience Ireland is on foot, at your own pace. No groups to keep up with. No rigid schedule. Just you, the trail, and the extraordinary Irish landscape. Walk 20km or stop at 10 — it's your call. But you're never truly alone: our team is a phone call away, your route is clearly marked, and your bags arrive before you do.

As Cliff likes to say: "Our guide shows the group when we'll get to the pub!" It's that spirit — equal parts adventure and enjoyment — that defines every Walking Holiday Ireland experience.
